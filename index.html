MLE

Modeling Language Engineering

Description

The Modeling Language Engineering (MLE) workshop aims at bringing together researchers and practitioners working on modeling language engineering. It is a meeting opportunity for Software Language Engineering (SLE) enthusiasts within the software modeling community.
